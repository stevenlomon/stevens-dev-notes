Welcome to my garden!

I do my best to follow two principles:
1. _**Your code is only ever as good as your mental models**_
2. _**Fuck the typical academic gatekeeping jargon :)**_

I'm a strong proponent of an idea and learning philosophy phrased incredibly well and eloquently by Naval Ravikant:  
**_If you understand something, you don't have to memorize it._**  
_**If you don't understand something, you don't want to memorize it.**_  
I wholeheartedly believe that focusing on first-principle comprehension over rote repetition is what makes learning stick long term

I love connecting the dots and seeing the big picture.  
I love synthesizing and crystallizing, comparing and contrasting, expanding and elaborating.   
All being tools for clarity and deeper intuitive understanding on the path to embodied mastery 🌱
